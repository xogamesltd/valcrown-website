Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use ValCrown:

  • Account information: Email address, full name, password (hashed — never stored in plain text)
  • Device information: A unique device identifier (VID) generated from your hardware fingerprint. We do not store personal hardware details.
  • License data: Your license key, plan type, activation date, expiry date
  • Usage data: Session timestamps, boost activations, AI queries (not stored permanently)
  • IP address: Logged for security and fraud prevention. Not sold or shared.
  • Payment data: Processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the ValCrown service
  • To validate your license and prevent fraud
  • To send important service emails (updates, license keys, support replies)
  • To improve the product based on anonymous usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (governed by Stripe's privacy policy)
  • Anthropic/Google: AI queries (anonymized, no personal data sent)
  • Cloudflare: DDoS protection and CDN
  • Law enforcement: Only when required by law

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by emailing support@valcrown.com. Payment records are retained for 7 years as required by financial regulations.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object to processing of your data

EU/EEA users: These rights are guaranteed under GDPR. To exercise them, email support@valcrown.com.

6. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for session management and language preferences. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. We use Cloudflare Turnstile for bot protection, which may set functional cookies.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including bcrypt password hashing, JWT token authentication, HTTPS encryption, and Cloudflare WAF protection. Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure.

8. Contact

Privacy questions: support@valcrown.com
