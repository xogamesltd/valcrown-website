ℹ️ About Device Binding
Your ValCrown license is bound to one Windows PC at a time. This prevents sharing and protects your account.
  • You can reset your device binding once every 30 days
  • After reset, your next login on any PC will activate it
  • If you need an emergency reset, contact support@valcrown.com
