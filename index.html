The Problem

Cloud gaming has unique bottlenecks.
Generic boosters miss all of them.

Traditional boosters optimize GPU settings that cloud games don't even use. Cloud gaming performance depends on your video decoder, your network quality, and your CPU staying cool under sustained load. ValCrown tackles all three.

📺
Video Decoder Priority
Cloud gaming is decode-heavy, not render-heavy. ValCrown frees CPU resources specifically for your video decoder — making streams smooth even on mid-range hardware.
🌐
Network Traffic Priority
Game data gets bandwidth priority over background updates and cloud syncs. DNS optimized to nearest cloud gaming edge servers for lowest possible latency.
🌡️
Thermal Management
Extended gaming causes heat throttling — your smooth 60fps drops to 30fps mid-game. ValCrown monitors temperature and warns you before throttling kicks in.
