EU Consumer Rights

If you are based in the EU/EEA, you have a statutory 14-day right of withdrawal for digital products under the Consumer Rights Directive, provided you have not downloaded and activated the product. Once a license key is redeemed, the right of withdrawal may not apply as per Article 16(m) of the Directive. Our 7-day policy is more generous and applies regardless.
