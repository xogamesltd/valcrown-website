Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

How does the 3-day free trial work?
Download ValCrown, create a free account, and you get 3 days of full access. No credit card needed. After 3 days, upgrade to continue using it.
What's the difference between 3-day and 14-day trials?
The 3-day trial is completely free — no card needed. The 14-day trial costs a small one-time fee but gives you 14 full days to test every feature thoroughly.
How do I activate my license after purchase?
After payment, your license key is shown on the success page. Download ValCrown, open the app, sign in, and enter the key to activate on your device.
Can I use ValCrown on multiple PCs?
Each license activates on 1 device. Need to transfer to a new PC? Use the self-service device reset in app settings (once every 30 days) or contact support@valcrown.com.
Does it work with cloud gaming platforms?
Yes — ValCrown has specialized Cloud Mode for GeForce NOW, Xbox Cloud Gaming, Boosteroid, Shadow PC, and Amazon Luna. It prioritizes the video decoder pipeline specifically.
What is the refund policy?
7-day refund on all paid plans, no questions asked. Email support@valcrown.com with your order email and we process it within 24 hours.
What Windows versions are supported?
Windows 10 and Windows 11 (64-bit only). Administrator privileges required for the boost engine and network features.
Is the Lifetime plan really lifetime?
Yes — one payment, never pay again. Includes all updates for the life of the product. This is the best value if you game regularly.
