Frequently asked questions
How does the 3-day free trial work?
Download ValCrown, create a free account, and you get 3 days of full access — no credit card needed. After 3 days, upgrade to continue. No automatic charges.
What's the difference between free and 14-day trials?
The 3-day trial is completely free — no card. The 14-day trial costs a small one-time fee and gives you 14 full days plus session history and Discord presence.
How do I get my license key after purchase?
Your key appears instantly on the success page after payment. It's also emailed to you and visible in your account dashboard at any time.
Can I use ValCrown on multiple PCs?
Each license activates on 1 device. You can reset to a new device yourself from app settings — once every 30 days. No support ticket needed.
Is the Lifetime plan really lifetime?
Yes — one payment, never pay again. You get all future updates for the life of the product. This is the best value if you game regularly.
What's your refund policy?
7-day full refund on all paid plans, no questions asked. Email support@valcrown.com with your order email and we process it within 24 hours.
Does it work with cloud gaming specifically?
Yes — ValCrown is built specifically for GeForce NOW, Xbox Cloud Gaming, Boosteroid, Shadow PC, Amazon Luna, and all major cloud platforms.
What Windows versions are supported?
Windows 10 and Windows 11 (64-bit only). Administrator privileges are required for the boost engine and network optimization features.
